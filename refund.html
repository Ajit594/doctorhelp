Refund Policy

Last Updated: February 2, 2026

  • Users can cancel an appointment at least 3 hours before the scheduled time.
  • Cancellation before service start: refund possible.
  • On valid cancellation, only 50% of the paid amount will be refunded.
  • Remaining amount will be deducted as service and processing charges.
  • Cancellation after service start or no-show: no refund.
  • Cancellation less than 3 hours before appointment is not allowed.
  • Surgery and insurance cancellations follow the service provider's policy.
  • Eligible refunds are processed within 5–7 working days to the original payment method.
  • Refund delays may occur due to Cashfree processing timelines.
  • Failed payments are automatically reversed.
  • Refund is not mandatory if delay is caused by the service provider.
